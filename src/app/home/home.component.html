Summary of Soma Nyumbani

Soma Nyumbani is supporting teachers in the Community Based Learning programme by producing ready-to-deliver session plans, designed to cover all the requirements outlined in the official documentation published by the MOE and TSC. Sessions are planned to take one hour each and are grouped into 4 daily sessions. However, we recommend teachers to be flexible and adapt the timings to their individual group of students’ needs. Sessions will be published on a regular basis making it easy for teachers to access. The New Sessions [link to New Sessions page] can be found below and a list of all the sessions published is available in the All Sessions [link to All Sessions page] section.
